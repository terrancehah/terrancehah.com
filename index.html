Hi, I am Terrance!

A tuition centre co-founder, and a self-teaching programmer.

I used to major in International Business. After I graduated, I started to engage in the educational field.

After serving in the field for a long time, I realised that there are much waiting to be fixed. A lot of our current education system just doesn't work.

Kids are only trained to be good employees. Nobody asked if they enjoy learning in school. Scores matter the most than before. Talents besides academic performances are not appreciated.

And there is a trend of technology addiction on kids. I started to think, instead of stopping kids from using phones and computers, why not embrace the power of technology?

So here I am, self-teaching to be a programmer as a teacher. I strive to empower the children with the power of technology.
